FitOutFit
Point a camera at your wardrobe — image segmentation detects every garment, the Fit Logic Engine suggests combinations, and a Cultural Respect module ensures you dress across traditions with authenticity, not appropriation.
The Problem

Putting together outfits is surprisingly hard — especially when exploring styles from other cultures. Fear of committing a fashion mistake, or worse, a cultural one, keeps people from expressing themselves. Generic styling apps ignore cultural context entirely.

The Engine

Live camera feed → CV segmentation detects garment type, colour, and style. A recommendation engine accumulates taste preferences over time. A Cultural Module validates combinations against a library of cultural dress standards and flags authenticity concerns — ensuring cross-cultural dressing is respectful, not accidental.
