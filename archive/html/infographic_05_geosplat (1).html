GeoSplat
Photorealistic 4D digital twins of mining sites — drone footage reconstructed into a live volumetric model that filters out moving equipment and calculates extracted ore volumes automatically.
The Problem

Tracking ore volumes on active mining sites is slow, error-prone, and disrupted by dust, vehicles, and constantly shifting terrain. Manual surveys are expensive and infrequent — leaving operators blind to real-time extraction rates and profitability.

The Engine

A drone fleet captures 4K video + GPS. 4D Gaussian Splatting reconstructs a photorealistic 3D scene, filtering dynamic objects (vehicles, personnel). Temporal diff between scans computes volumetric delta. Volume converts directly to estimated profit per tonne — surfaced on a WebGL dashboard.
