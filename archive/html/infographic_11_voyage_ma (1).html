Voyage.ma
Any point A to any point B in Morocco — train, collective taxi, private — unified under one real-time journey plan, with AI delay prediction and the scale of the 2030 World Cup already in mind.
The Problem

ONCF train delays are unpredictable. Collective taxis wait until full before departing — passengers sit stranded, uncertain. No unified platform coordinates Morocco's mixed transport modes. For tourists and intercity travellers, planning a journey across Morocco is a chain of guesses. The 2030 World Cup will expose this at global scale.

The Engine

An ONCF scraper pulls live and historical train data to train a delay predictor. A Seat Orchestrator manages collective taxi availability in real time — drivers declare seats, passengers book, taxis depart full. A multi-modal routing engine combines train + taxi + private legs into a single end-to-end journey plan, rerouting dynamically when delays are predicted.
