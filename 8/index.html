TheRandom App
Structured randomness that breaks decision paralysis and resurfaces neglected social connections — because flipping a coin doesn't give you the answer, it forces you to react to one, and that reaction is the answer.
The Problem

Routine breeds habituation — fixed alarms are tuned out, repeated decisions cause fatigue, and social bonds quietly atrophy as life accelerates. Productivity apps add more structure. This adds less: a calculated dose of unpredictability that wakes up the brain and the heart.

The Engine

A Probabilistic Scheduler takes user-defined constraints (wake windows, social contact frequency, donation range, activity library) and fires three modules: Variable Alarm (anti-habituation wake-up), Decision Catalyst (coin-flip for paralysis), and Social & Altruism (randomised reminders to call a parent, message a friend, make a small donation).
