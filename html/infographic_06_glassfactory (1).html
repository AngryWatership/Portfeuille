GlassFactory
A local Edge-AI monitors production lines in real time and gives factory clients a live transparency feed — turning logistics into a marketing advantage, with data that never leaves the factory floor.
The Problem

Unplanned production stoppages are costly and unpredictable. Clients have no visibility into the status of their orders. Sending data to the cloud raises confidentiality concerns for manufacturers — yet on-site expertise is expensive and unavailable around the clock.

The Engine

IP cameras feed RTSP streams to a local edge server — no cloud. YOLO detects anomalies against a learned baseline. A local LLM with RAG over technical manuals generates maintenance action plans. Alerts route to technicians; a filtered read-only view goes to clients. Proof of Live: clients press emoji buttons in their portal — physical actuators stamp the mark on the glass in front of the camera, providing an unforgeable liveness signal.
